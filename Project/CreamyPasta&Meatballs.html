Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 15 mins | Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Cuisine: American, Italian

Our creamy meatball pasta recipe is a simple combination of basic spices, homemade meatballs, cream, and wide noodles. This outcome if very similar to Alfredo without the risk of calling it that.

Credit: Cook n' Share

Ingredients:

1 pound lean ground beef
2 tablespoons ketchup
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1⁄2 teaspoon garlic powder
1⁄2 teaspoon salt
1⁄2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
